Plain Text Fixture
==================

This document exercises the loader's direct byte-to-text decoding path for the
".txt" extension. Nothing about it should require extraction: the bytes on the
wire are exactly the characters below.

Marker: FORMAT_MARKER_TXT

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Integer posuere erat a
ante venenatis dapibus posuere velit aliquet. Cras justo odio, dapibus ac
facilisis in, egestas eget quam. Vestibulum id ligula porta felis euismod semper.

Numbers survive too: 0123456789. Punctuation: !@#$%^&*()_+-=[]{};:'",.<>/?
